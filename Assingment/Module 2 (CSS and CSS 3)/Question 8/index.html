Question 8 : What is the use of the background-position property?

CSS background-position property are use to set a background image position in particular element.
Control the horizontal and vertical positioning of the background image.

background-position values:

top left :- top left corner of the element. this is default value.
top center :- top center of the element.
top right :- top right corner of the element.
center left :- center left of the element.
center center :- center of the element.
center right :- center right of the element.
bottom left :- bottom left corner of the element.
bottom center :- bottom center of the element.
bottom right :- bottom right corner of the element.
